01

Practice Area

Ground-Up Commercial Construction

We take projects from raw land to certificate of occupancy. Our pre-construction process means no surprises at the permit stage — by the time a shovel breaks ground, every contingency has been mapped, priced, and accounted for. We've delivered everything from 5-story medical office buildings to 500,000 sq ft distribution facilities, and we bring the same level of discipline to both.

Key Deliverables

Site analysis & feasibility Permitting & entitlements Foundation through envelope MEP coordination Commissioning & handover

Industries:  Healthcare  ·  Corporate Office  ·  Logistics  ·  Government

02

Practice Area

Tenant Improvement & Fit-Outs

The interior matters as much as the structure. We specialize in high-finish corporate TI work across active multi-tenant buildings, with minimal disruption to neighboring tenants. Our scheduling protocols are built around occupied environments — we know how to get a 40,000 sq ft floor plate done without shutting down the elevator bank for three weeks.

Key Deliverables

Existing conditions assessment Space planning & design coordination Phased construction in occupied buildings Custom millwork & high-spec finishes FF&E coordination

Industries:  Financial Services  ·  Law & Professional  ·  Technology  ·  Healthcare

03

Practice Area

Design-Build Delivery

When schedule compression is the primary constraint, design-build is the right tool. We hold both the design and construction contract, which means the architect's decisions and the superintendent's reality are always in the same room. Clients get a single point of accountability and a process that moves faster because there's no agency boundary to navigate between design and build.

Key Deliverables

Unified design & construction contract Concept through construction documentation Early procurement & long-lead coordination Integrated cost & schedule management Single-source owner accountability

Industries:  Industrial  ·  Retail  ·  Mixed-Use  ·  Municipal

04

Practice Area

Retail Rollout Programs

National brands trust us to execute their store programs at scale without drift in quality or timeline. We've managed concurrent rollouts across 8 markets simultaneously, with consistent scopes, standardized reporting, and a dedicated retail program team that doesn't treat each location like it's the first one they've ever built. Prototypes delivered on time become rollouts delivered on budget.

Key Deliverables

Prototype development & value engineering Multi-site program management Landlord coordination & permit management Fixture & equipment installation Standardized reporting dashboards

Industries:  QSR & Food Service  ·  Apparel  ·  Financial  ·  Specialty Retail

05

Practice Area

Industrial & Warehouse Construction

Large footprint, fast track, and zero tolerance for delays in a space where tenant operations depend on opening dates. From tilt-up distribution centers to climate-controlled manufacturing facilities, we have the self-perform capacity and the logistics expertise to keep industrial projects moving — even when the supply chain doesn't.

Key Deliverables

Tilt-up & pre-engineered metal buildings Site development & utility coordination Dock & drive-in door systems HVAC, electrical & compressed air systems Racking & material handling coordination

Industries:  E-Commerce & Logistics  ·  Manufacturing  ·  Cold Storage  ·  Government

06

Practice Area

Sustainability-Led Projects

We don't treat LEED certification as an add-on you pay extra for. It's baked into how we plan and build. Our sustainability team, led by Angela Tran, is involved from day one of pre-construction — which means green strategies get priced and sequenced correctly instead of value-engineered out at the last minute. 40% of our last three years of project volume has achieved LEED certification. That number is not accidental.

Key Deliverables

LEED documentation & certification management Energy modeling coordination Sustainable material sourcing Waste diversion & recycling programs LEED AP team on every qualifying project

Industries:  Corporate Office  ·  Healthcare  ·  Municipal  ·  Mixed-Use
