Service 01

Custom Home Building

Building a custom home is one of the most significant investments a family can make — and we treat it that way. Every project begins with an in-depth consultation where we listen more than we talk. We learn how you live, what you value, and what your home needs to feel like yours.

From there, we work closely with your architect (or connect you with one of our trusted partners) to translate your vision into a detailed plan. Robert Jr. personally oversees every custom build from foundation pour to final walk-through, ensuring that not a single detail falls through the cracks.

  • Site evaluation & preparation
  • Foundation, framing & structural work
  • Electrical, plumbing & HVAC coordination
  • Insulation, drywall, flooring & cabinetry
  • Interior & exterior finish selections
  • Final inspection & owner walkthrough

Service 03

Room Additions

Your family grows. Your needs change. Sometimes your house just needs more room. A well-designed addition — when done right — feels like it was always part of the home. That seamless integration is what we specialize in: matching existing architecture, materials, and proportions so the new space never feels tacked on.

We handle every aspect of the addition, from pulling permits to pouring the new foundation, from framing to the final coat of paint. Whether it's a master suite, a home office, a sunroom, or an in-law apartment, we build it as carefully as we'd build the whole home.

  • Master bedroom & bathroom suites
  • Home offices & bonus rooms
  • In-law suites & accessory dwelling units
  • Sunrooms & screened porches
  • Garage conversions & expansions

Service 04

Deck & Outdoor Structures

Western North Carolina was made to be lived outdoors. A well-built deck, porch, or pergola doesn't just add square footage — it extends your home into the landscape and becomes the place where your family actually spends time together. We build outdoor structures designed to hold up against mountain weather and look better every year.

We work with a range of materials — pressure-treated lumber, cedar, composite decking, and reclaimed wood — and we'll help you choose what's right for your climate, budget, and aesthetic. Every structure is built to code, properly footed, and finished to the same standard as our interior work.

  • Custom decks (single & multi-level)
  • Wraparound & covered porches
  • Pergolas, gazebos & outdoor pavilions
  • Screened-in porches & three-season rooms
  • Outdoor kitchens & fire pit areas
  • Retaining walls & hardscaping

Service 05

Historic Home Restoration

The Blue Ridge Mountains are home to some of the most beautiful historic properties in the South — and they deserve builders who understand what they're working with. Our historic restoration work is guided by a deep respect for original materials, period craftsmanship, and the stories embedded in every old-growth beam and hand-set brick.

We work on homes from the late 1800s through mid-century, restoring structural integrity while carefully preserving or replicating historic details. We're EPA Lead-Safe certified, experienced with historic tax credit projects, and familiar with local preservation guidelines in the Asheville area.

  • Structural stabilization & foundation repair
  • Original millwork repair & replication
  • Window & door restoration or period-match replacement
  • Lead-safe renovation (EPA certified)
  • Historic tax credit project experience
  • Modern systems integration (HVAC, electrical, plumbing)
