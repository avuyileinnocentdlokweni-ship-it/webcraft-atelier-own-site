Our Story

The Beginning

A Handshake and a Pickup Truck

In 1987, Robert Harven Sr. shook his neighbor's hand, loaded up his pickup truck with a few basic tools, and poured his entire savings into starting a construction business in Asheville, North Carolina. He had no office, no website, and no marketing budget — just an unshakeable belief that doing quality work and treating people right would carry him through.

It did. Word spread fast. Small repairs turned into full kitchen renovations, and full renovations turned into ground-up custom homes. By the early 1990s, Harven Construction had established itself as a trusted name across Buncombe County — a reputation earned one satisfied homeowner at a time.

A New Generation

Sons Join the Family Business

Robert Jr. picked up his first hammer at age nine on a job site beside his father. By the time he graduated high school, he had already framed two additions and learned to read blueprints. He joined the business full-time in 2001, bringing fresh energy and a deep respect for the old-school methods that had built the company's reputation.

Dale followed in 2004, bringing his natural gift for logistics and people management. Where Robert Jr. thrives on the technical precision of a build, Dale has a rare ability to keep multiple project sites running smoothly — on schedule, on budget, and to the standard the Harven name demands. Together, the brothers have pushed the company into a new era without sacrificing an ounce of the character it was built on.

Today

A Trusted Name Across Three Counties

Today, Harven & Sons Construction is one of the most recognized custom home builders and renovation specialists in Western North Carolina. The company serves families across Buncombe, Henderson, and Transylvania counties, taking on everything from mountain retreats in Weaverville to historic farmhouse restorations in Flat Rock.

The secret hasn't changed. Robert Sr. still stops by job sites. Robert Jr. still walks every framing inspection himself. Dale still answers his phone on weekends. And Linda, who joined the family business in 2008, makes sure every client feels heard, informed, and confident throughout the entire process. That's what a family business looks like — and that's what sets Harven & Sons apart.
