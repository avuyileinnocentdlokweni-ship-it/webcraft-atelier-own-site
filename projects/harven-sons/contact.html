Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

How long does a custom home build take?

Timelines vary by project size and complexity, but most custom homes take between 8 and 14 months from permit approval to final walkthrough. We'll give you a realistic schedule during your consultation and keep you updated throughout the build.

Do you work with architects, or do I need to hire one separately?

We have long-standing relationships with several architects in the Asheville area and are happy to connect you with the right fit. That said, we can also work with any architect you've already chosen — our job is to build the vision, not own it.

What counties do you serve?

Our primary service area includes Buncombe, Henderson, and Transylvania counties. We also take select projects in surrounding areas depending on scope — just ask and we'll let you know.

Is a deposit required to start a project?

Yes — we require a deposit before mobilizing on any project. The amount and payment schedule are outlined clearly in your written contract before work begins. We never ask for large upfront sums; payments are tied to project milestones.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We hold NC General Contractor License #44821 and carry full general liability and workers' compensation insurance on every project. We're also EPA Lead-Safe Certified for older home work. Documentation is available upon request.
