Our Story

A Family Name You Can Trust

In 1987, Robert Harven Sr. picked up a hammer, shook a neighbor's hand, and built his first addition on a quiet street in Asheville. With nothing but a pickup truck and a relentless work ethic, he grew what started as small repair jobs into a full-scale custom home building operation trusted across Western North Carolina.

Today, Robert's sons — Robert Jr. and Dale — carry the business forward with the same old-school values their father instilled: show up on time, do the work right the first time, and treat every client's home as if it were your own. Robert Jr. leads custom builds from foundation to finish, while Dale runs daily operations and quality control across every active project site.

The Harven name has become synonymous with quality in Buncombe, Henderson, and Transylvania counties. From mountain retreats in Weaverville to lakefront renovations in Lake Lure, every project carries the weight of a family legacy — and the pride of genuine craftsmanship.
